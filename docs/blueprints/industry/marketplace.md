# Marketplace Blueprint

> Additional checklist for two-sided marketplace SaaS (buyers + sellers/providers).
> Use alongside the core 15 blueprints — this covers marketplace-specific blind spots.

## Supply Side (Sellers/Providers)

- [ ] [P0] Seller onboarding flow with identity verification.
- [ ] [P0] Seller dashboard: listings, orders, earnings, payouts.
- [ ] [P0] Listing creation with required fields, images, and pricing.
- [ ] [P1] Listing moderation workflow (auto-flag + manual review).
- [ ] [P1] Seller ratings and review system.
- [ ] [P1] Bulk listing management (import CSV, duplicate, archive).
- [ ] [P2] Seller analytics (views, conversion rate, revenue trends).
- [ ] [P2] Seller tier system (badges, featured placement, reduced fees).

## Demand Side (Buyers)

- [ ] [P0] Search with filters, sorting, and pagination.
- [ ] [P0] Product/service detail page with seller info and reviews.
- [ ] [P1] Saved items / wishlist functionality.
- [ ] [P1] Buyer protection policy clearly displayed.
- [ ] [P1] Recommendation engine or "similar items" section.
- [ ] [P2] Buyer purchase history and reorder capability.
- [ ] [P2] Price alerts or notifications for watched items.

## Matching & Discovery

- [ ] [P0] Category taxonomy defined and navigable.
- [ ] [P1] Search autocomplete with popular queries.
- [ ] [P1] Location-based filtering (if applicable).
- [ ] [P1] Featured/promoted listings mechanism.
- [ ] [P2] Personalized feed based on browsing history.
- [ ] [P2] SEO-optimized category and listing pages.

## Transactions & Payments

- [ ] [P0] Escrow or held payment until delivery confirmed.
- [ ] [P0] Split payment (platform fee + seller payout) via Stripe Connect or equivalent.
- [ ] [P0] Refund and dispute resolution process defined.
- [ ] [P1] Multiple payment methods (card, PayPal, local methods).
- [ ] [P1] Automated payout schedule for sellers (weekly/monthly).
- [ ] [P1] Invoice generation for both buyers and sellers.
- [ ] [P2] Multi-currency support for international marketplaces.
- [ ] [P2] Tax calculation and reporting (1099-K for US sellers).

## Trust & Safety

- [ ] [P0] User verification (email, phone, ID for high-value transactions).
- [ ] [P0] Report/flag mechanism for listings and users.
- [ ] [P0] Content moderation policy published.
- [ ] [P1] Fraud detection: duplicate listings, fake reviews, payment fraud.
- [ ] [P1] Blocked user management (seller can block buyer and vice versa).
- [ ] [P1] Transaction history for dispute evidence.
- [ ] [P2] Identity verification badges ("Verified Seller").
- [ ] [P2] Automated scam detection (suspicious pricing, stolen images).

## Communication

- [ ] [P0] In-app messaging between buyer and seller.
- [ ] [P1] Message templates for common interactions.
- [ ] [P1] Notification system: new orders, messages, reviews, payouts.
- [ ] [P1] Response time tracking (displayed on seller profile).
- [ ] [P2] Automated follow-up messages (order confirmation, delivery reminder).

## Marketplace Economics

- [ ] [P0] Commission/fee structure clearly displayed to sellers.
- [ ] [P1] Pricing guidance for sellers (market average, competitive range).
- [ ] [P1] Minimum viable liquidity plan (enough supply before marketing demand).
- [ ] [P2] Dynamic pricing or surge pricing (if applicable).
- [ ] [P2] Loyalty or rewards program for repeat buyers.

## Legal & Compliance

- [ ] [P0] Terms of service covering platform liability limitations.
- [ ] [P0] Seller agreement / merchant terms of service.
- [ ] [P1] Sales tax / VAT handling for marketplace transactions.
- [ ] [P1] KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance for seller payouts.
- [ ] [P2] Cross-border trade compliance (import/export, restricted goods).
- [ ] [P2] Insurance or guarantee program for high-value transactions.
