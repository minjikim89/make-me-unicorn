# Billing and Tax Checklist

## Pricing and Plans

- [ ] Plan limits and entitlements are explicit.
- [ ] Upgrade/downgrade behavior is defined.
- [ ] Trial policy is documented.

## Checkout

- [ ] Success and cancel redirects are implemented.
- [ ] Payment failure retry/notification path exists.
- [ ] Payment method update flow exists.

## Webhook and State

- [ ] Webhook signatures are validated.
- [ ] Webhook handling is idempotent.
- [ ] Subscription state updates access control.

## Refund and Cancellation

- [ ] Immediate vs period-end cancellation policy is defined.
- [ ] Refund criteria and exceptions are documented.
- [ ] Chargeback/dispute workflow is defined.

## Tax and Invoice

- [ ] Tax/VAT responsibility is defined.
- [ ] Invoice/receipt delivery path is working.
- [ ] Required legal business info is published.
