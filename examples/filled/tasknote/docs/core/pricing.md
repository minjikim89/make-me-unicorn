# Pricing

## Pricing Philosophy

- Value-based pricing principle: charge for execution leverage and reduced risk.
- Role of free plan: validate workflow fit and habit adoption.

## Plans

| Plan | Target User | Price | Limits | Included Features |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | solo explorer | $0 | 1 project, 30-day history | core board + checklist |
| Pro | shipping founder | $19/mo | 5 projects | billing checks, decision history |
| Team | small studio | $59/mo | 20 projects | shared gates and governance |

## Billing Rules

- Billing cycle: monthly
- Upgrade/downgrade policy: immediate upgrade, next-cycle downgrade
- Cancellation policy: period-end cancellation
- Refund policy: first 7 days for first-time subscribers

## Revenue Experiments

- Hypothesis: annual plan increases retention and upfront commitment.
- Experiment design: show annual CTA after week-2 activation.
- Success criteria: +20% paid retention at week 8.
